The following categories of passengers shall be permitted to enter Gaza International Airport without visas:
  • Palestinian Citizens of the West Bank and Gaza Strip holding Palestinian ID Cards, or Palestinian Passports issued by the Palestinian Authority.
  • Visitors to Gaza Strip or the West Bank holding valid visit permits, provided their passports are valid for at least 6 months.
  • Israeli Citizens holding Israeli Passports or Laissez-Passer.
  • Nationals holding Passports issued by the Countries listed in (Attachment "A").

All other Categories shall not be permitted to enter Gaza Int'l Airport unless holding a valid visa issued by an Israeli Embassy and their passports are valid for at least 6 months.
