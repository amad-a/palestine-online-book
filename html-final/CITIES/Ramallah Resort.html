Ramallah is a renowned mountainous summer resort lies just 15 km to the north of Jerusalem. It's summer nights are pleasantly cool and invite visitors from all over the country to enjoy its lively outdoor restaurants and cafes. Ramallah with its central location has become the choice of many International banks and companies to set their offices at. This is return has contributed to the influx of visitors from all over the world to the city.
