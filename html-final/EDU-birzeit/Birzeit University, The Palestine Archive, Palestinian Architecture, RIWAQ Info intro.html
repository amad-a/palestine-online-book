Virtual Exhibition Introduction

On one level, Netscape was made for this Virtual Exhibition, as the <BODY BACKGROUND> command allows for a tiling effect to cover the whole page. Unfortunately, some of the tile images in the Virtual Exhibition are corner sections. Border patterns will therefore be removed from the background to allow a better tiling effect.

The first two tiles are from the Nablus area, the remaining ten from the Ramallah area.
