  • ABOUT BIRZEIT
    Birzeit University is an independent Palestinian institution, situated 20 km north of Jerusalem, in the Israeli-occupied West Bank. Founded as a school in 1924, Birzeit began its university programme in 1972 and is currently introducing a masters degree programme. The Birzeit website, launched 6 June 1996, is Netscape-enhanced for all versions 1.1 and after. We are working on making the website Microsoft Internet Explorer friendly as well.     French language, German Language and text only websites are available with brief information about Birzeit University.
